About FoodResQ

FoodResQ is a community-driven platform built to bridge the gap between food waste and hunger. Our mission is to rescue surplus food from restaurants, households, and events and redirect it to those in need‚Äîsafely and efficiently.

Developed by a passionate team of student developers, FoodResQ empowers food donors and volunteers through a simple, intuitive interface. Donors can post excess food with just a few clicks, while nearby volunteers can view and pick up donations for timely delivery to shelters, NGOs, or individuals.

We believe that no edible food should go to waste while people go hungry. By leveraging technology for social good, FoodResQ aims to create a more sustainable and compassionate society‚Äîone meal at a time.
